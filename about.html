The idea behind the Axion.

The primary goal of AxionOS is to deliver a smooth and refined Android experience. We aspire to be the open-source equivalent of NothingOS. By reverse-engineering core components of NothingOS, we aim to recreate and improve upon them as part of our open-source initiative—piece by piece—until we develop our own distinct framework.

Our vision explores the question: what if NothingOS were more expressive? What if it embraced Material You design, and more? Through this lens, we craft a unique "what if" version of the original. Additionally, we strive to incorporate OEM-level optimizations into AOSP, offering users a more flexible and performance-oriented experience.
